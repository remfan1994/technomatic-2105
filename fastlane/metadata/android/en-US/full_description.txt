Radio Breaker generates synthetic electronic music directly on your Android device.

It is built as a minimalist generated-music player: press PLAY, let the engine create a track, press NEXT when you want a new generated composition, or set TRACK SECONDS for longer pieces.

The music engine uses procedural drums, bass, leads, motifs, sections, hooks, breakdowns, and returns. It uses no samples and does not try to imitate acoustic instruments.

Privacy and freedom properties:

* no Internet permission
* no accounts
* no advertising SDKs
* no analytics
* no trackers
* no external sound assets
* no sample packs

Radio Breaker is experimental. Its purpose is to explore on-device electronic music generation as a replacement for passive algorithmic radio.
